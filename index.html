About Us

Read our story

We've been serving the Market with Pride Since 1955

We have been in the market experiencing six decades. We started with a very small shop in Amravati, and today it has become one of the leading distributor of foodgrains in Vidharbha.

Being in the centre of India (Near Nagpur), ASHOKKUMAR AJESHKUMAR SAHU has a nationwide reach and we are aiming to expand it further.

Currently the third generation of the firm manages the business and the fourth generation is sharpening their skills under their guidance.

A.A.SAHU has three divisions.


1. AJESHKUMAR SAHU trades in wholesale in all food grains.


2. B.N.SAHU is in retail trading, is a chief supplier to all prominent hotels restaurant and caterers in Amravati city.


3. ASHI FOOD PRODUCTS is our pulses manufacturing unit.
